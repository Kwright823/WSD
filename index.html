Figure skating is a sport and activity in which individuals, duos, or groups perform on figure skates on ice.
In intermediate through senior-level competition, skaters generally perform two programs (short and long) which, depending on the discipline, may include spins, jumps, moves in the field, lifts, throw jumps, death spirals, and other elements or moves.
